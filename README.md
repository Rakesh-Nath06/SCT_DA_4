# SCT_DA_4 

## Marketing Campaign Business Insights Report (EDA)

## Project Overview

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a marketing campaign performance dataset to uncover actionable business insights, optimize marketing spend, and recommend strategic budget allocation based on ROI.

Rather than building machine learning models, this analysis emphasizes understanding why certain marketing channels perform better, identifying inefficiencies in the customer funnel, and supporting executive decision-making through business intelligence.

## Objective
Clean and preprocess raw marketing campaign data
Analyze campaign performance across channels
Visualize customer conversion funnel
Calculate key marketing KPIs
Evaluate ROI and CPA efficiency
Build an interactive Power BI dashboard
Provide strategic business recommendations for budget optimization

## Key Insights
Google Ads delivered the highest ROI and strongest revenue performance.
Facebook showed high spending but weaker profitability.
Major funnel drop-offs between clicks and leads indicate landing page inefficiencies.
Email marketing achieved the lowest CPA with strong conversion efficiency.
Strategic Recommendations
Increase budget allocation for Google Ads.
Expand Email Marketing investment.
Optimize Instagram campaigns before scaling further.
Reduce or restructure Facebook ad spend.
